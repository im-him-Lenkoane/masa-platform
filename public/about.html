About M@SA

Messelaar @ STEM Academy NPC is a community-driven non-profit organisation dedicated to dismantling educational barriers in South Africa. We believe every learner deserves free, world-class STEM education — no registration, no fees, no gatekeeping.

Our Pillars
🎯

Our Mission

To provide free, high-quality, curriculum-aligned STEM education to all South African learners from Grade 10 through to tertiary level.

🌍

Community First

Born from the Messelaar community, our programmes are designed with local context and challenges in mind, building education from the ground up.

🔓

Open Access

No accounts. No fees. No gatekeeping. Every resource is immediately accessible to any learner, anywhere in South Africa and beyond.

🤖

AI-Assisted Learning

Our integrated AI tutor adapts its responses to each student's exact level — Grade 10 learners and university students get appropriately calibrated help.

📶

Offline Ready

Subject content can be downloaded to any device for offline study — critical for learners with limited or unreliable internet access.

🇿🇦

South African Context

Everything is aligned to the South African CAPS curriculum and framed within local examples, industries, and opportunities.

The Platform

M@SA serves two student paths: School Learners in Grades 10–12 following the CAPS curriculum, and Tertiary Students across faculties including Engineering, Natural Sciences, Health Sciences, Information Technology, Built Environment, and Agricultural Sciences. Both paths lead to dedicated subject pages packed with videos, study notes, PDFs, practice questions, quizzes, and AI-assisted tutoring.
